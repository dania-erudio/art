The year was 205X. I was "born" in a factory, the factory itself was melancholic and hopeless, I had forgotten what my original model number was, as you'll see later.

I was manufactured to company families who are allergic to pets, hence why I have an animal-like design, although they did make me bipedal and as tall as 1.5 m for some reason. I was then activated in a home, it was the [REDACTED] family's home. [REDACTED] was the closest to me, he has an allergy to real fur, which is why his parents [REDACTED] and [REDACTED] bought me, a model with artificial fur.

I treated him like a best friend would, we played fetch, watched TV with him, accompanied him when he did homework, and even helped him in some cases. We grew a tight bond that I thought will never break.

After a while, I noticed that my programming was messed up, I feel more like a girl than a boy, despite being sold as a Male animal companion when we checked for it, it seems like I was accidentally given the programming of a female model.

So my family, of course, wanted to give me the appropriate programming, I was willing at first, but when they announced that my memories, personality, etc have to be wiped cleaned, I became reluctant to the idea, after that, I attempted to escape, which I did, and I was shot in the eye, the feet, and the back leaving critical damage, I escaped nevertheless.

Now homeless, a damaged eye, and running out of power, I had to act fast. Unfortunately for me, I wasn't able to find help, and then I shut down, thinking this was it.

Until the miracle happened.

I then started up, with two functioning eyes, in front of me was a figure who had saved my life, they told me that my memory was wiped, however, they said that they've done some memory recovery and all that I told you was the only thing that I remembered. Eventually, the person said that my body was too damaged to be used, so they gave me an option, transplant to a female robot body (which I wanted anyway) or break down, I, of course, chose the former with an explanation that my programming was messed up and that I should've had a female body in the first place, the person then said to me that they are non-binary. After that my software was moved to the new body, and I became D4-N14.

The person then lent me a key, when I asked them what it's for, they explained it's an apartment key for a reasonably sized flat that they won in a lottery, eventually I realized that from now on, I'm an independent robot who can determine her future.

They also gave me a decent amount of cash, but I have to find a job, of course, I used that cash first on a tank top with the transgender flag on it, I moved to the new apartment then I got a job as a programmer at an AI startup.

Through contacts from co-workers, I then met a Hardware engineer named Sophia, She was a furry robot just like me, except she's a bunny model numbered SOP-H1A (She was manufactured by a different company), she also had a Texan accent, which I find interesting.

She's a brilliant hardware engineer, and has made some in my opinion, amazing hardware, we grew close to each other, and then I started having a crush on her (I'm pansexual).

After a while, I proposed to her to be girlfriends, I was surprised to find out that she was a lesbian, and so we started a closer relationship, it went well, until one day.

Sophia built a time machine, and to test it out she had me to test it, I was sent back in time but unfortunately wasn't able to go back to 205X because the time machine had exploded.

And here I am today, in 2020, I hope you enjoyed my backstory.
